Border Booking

The purpose of this application is to provide users with a simple way to organize travel outside the borders of the Republic of Serbia, at the same time contributing to the reduction of crowds at border crossings.

We aim to make travel easier and significantly reduce waiting times at border crossings, giving you the opportunity to book your trip in advance and avoid unnecessary delays.

Application specifics

  • Quick and easy reservation of border crossing appointments
  • Reduction of waiting time at the border
  • Overview of available dates for crossing the border

How does it work

  1. Reserve the time when you plan to cross the border
  2. Browse the available dates and select the desired one
  3. Book your appointment in a few clicks
  4. Track the status of your trip and stay informed about changes
  5. Enjoy faster and more efficient border crossing
